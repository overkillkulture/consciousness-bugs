3. **Be Specific**: Provide detailed steps to reproduce the issue
4. **Include Context**: Share your environment details (OS, browser, version)
5. **Add Evidence**: Screenshots, error logs, or code snippets are very helpful

## ğŸ“‹ What Makes a Good Bug Report?

A good bug report includes:
- **Clear Title**: Short, descriptive summary (e.g., "Dashboard crashes when loading large datasets")
- **Reproduction Steps**: Exact steps to trigger the bug
- **Expected vs Actual**: What should happen vs what actually happens
- **Environment**: Your setup (OS, browser, version)
- **Impact**: How severe is the issue?
